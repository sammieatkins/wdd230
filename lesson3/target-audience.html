Target Audience

Who: Any person wanting to see events going on in their city. Also people promoting a business or people who want to know more about businesses around them. Ages 10-100 who have internet access.

Personas

Occupation: Homemaker

Motivation to use the site: Wants to benefit from local businesses and to see upcoming events in the community.

Occupation: Small Business Owner

Motivation to use the site: Wants to promote his business and network with other businesses.
